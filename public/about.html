Our Story

We started AdScale Pro because we saw a massive problem – small businesses in India were being priced out of digital advertising.

Traditional agencies charge ₹25,000-₹1,00,000 per month just for management fees, on top of your ad spend. For a small business spending ₹20,000 on ads, paying another ₹30,000 in agency fees simply doesn't make sense.

We built AdScale Pro to change that. With our platform, you pay a one-time setup fee of just ₹999 and then every rupee you add goes directly to your ads. No hidden fees, no monthly retainers, no long-term contracts.

Our goal is simple – to help 10,000 MSMEs run profitable digital ad campaigns by 2025, without breaking the bank.

Our Mission

To make performance marketing accessible for every MSME and SME in India. We believe every business, regardless of size, deserves access to powerful digital advertising tools without paying exorbitant agency fees.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted performance marketing platform for small businesses. We envision a future where running ads is as simple as sending a WhatsApp message, and every business owner can see exactly where their money goes.

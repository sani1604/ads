FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about AdScale Pro.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

Traditional agencies charge monthly retainers (₹25,000-₹1,00,000) regardless of your ad spend. With AdScale Pro, you pay a one-time setup fee (starting ₹999) and then only your ad budget goes to actual ads. You get full transparency, a self-service dashboard, and no long-term contracts.

Who creates the ad creatives?

Our in-house design team creates all ad creatives based on your brand guidelines and industry best practices. Every creative is sent to your dashboard for approval before going live. You can request unlimited revisions.

What's the minimum ad budget I need?

For Starter plan: minimum ₹5,000/month, Growth plan: minimum ₹15,000/month, Scale plan: minimum ₹50,000/month. You can add more anytime through your wallet.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts. You can pause your campaigns, stop adding money to your wallet, or cancel completely anytime. Your remaining wallet balance can be refunded (minus any processing fees).

How do I see my leads?

All leads appear in real-time on your dashboard. You'll get instant notifications on email/WhatsApp. You can also export leads to Excel or connect to your existing CRM via our API.

What platforms do you advertise on?

We support Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube), LinkedIn, and Twitter/X. The platforms available depend on your chosen plan.
